CAFE Fines Improvement Act

Preamble Article I
Status Cosponsors House Senate Signatures

Preamble

As the United States, with the help of TREEPA, moves towards greener energy policy at home, we must not forget greener energy policy on the road. The aim of this bill is to further incentivize companies to manufacture more gas efficient vehicles. These improved standards would become effective immediately after Congressional passage and Presidential signature.

Article I: Fines

Section 1: Passenger Cars - Fines for Non-Compliance

Penalty should be applied to all passenger cars (model year 2019 and onwards) on the basis of $100 for every 1/10th of a mile per gallon below CAFE standards on every vehicle produced, with increases in fines laid out in Article I, Section III.

Section 2: Light Trucks - Fines for Non-Compliance

Penalty should be applied to all light trucks (model year 2019 and onwards) on the basis of $125 for every 1/10th of a mile per gallon below CAFE standards on every vehicle produced, with increases in fines laid out in Article I, Section III.

Section 3: Fine Increases

Penalties laid out in Article II, Sections I&II will be increased at a rate of 3.5% per year, through the year 2025, as shown in the table below.
