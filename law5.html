The Transparency Guarantee Act

Preamble Article I
Status Cosponsors Senate House Signatures

Preamble

As the country is inundated with concerns apropos secret government transactions and deals, this Transparency Guarantee Act ensures the transparency of the Congress, Bureaucracy and Supreme Court through the means of spectators on the government server. This bill is to be effective immediately once it has been passed.

Article I: Governmental Transparency

Section 1: Government Server

A permanent invite to the government server shall be available to all citizens of the discord. All deliberations, discussions and votes on the government server will be 100% available for anybody to read. As per the first amendment, any citizens have to right to protest and/or petition anything they want in the government server. Any unruly spectators (as per the first amendment and other common sense chat rules) may be removed as per the discretion of staff members.

Section 2: Judicial Process Transparency

In order to protect the opinions of the Supreme Court justices, they will not be forced to air every single opinion they have. However, all deliberations and discussions made in the government will be permitted to view to all on the government server.

Section 3: Abolition of the Main Discord Channels

In the past, a senate floor and house floor discord channel was made on the main channel. This was a temporary fix to the transparency issue. Now that all citizens are able to view the government server deliberations, which are more plentiful, there is no need for these artifacts of the past. These two discord channels are to be abolished, and the news channel and/or voting history channel will be used to release the voting history.
